Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What regions do you operate in?

We currently operate across Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and parts of Asia, with our headquarters in Dubai serving as the hub for all operations. Our network covers 25+ countries with local partners in each region.

How do you ensure quality of auto parts?

All auto parts go through a rigorous 3-stage quality check process: supplier verification, pre-shipment inspection, and final warehouse check. We only work with certified manufacturers and provide warranties on all parts.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We provide air, sea, and land freight options with various service levels from economy to express. Our logistics team will recommend the most cost-effective and timely solution based on your specific needs.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, we provide complete customs brokerage services including documentation preparation, duty calculation, and regulatory compliance. Our team stays updated on all trade agreements and tariff changes.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we serve clients across various sectors, our core expertise lies in automotive parts, industrial equipment, consumer electronics, and FMCG products. We've developed specialized supply chains for these industries.
